iPhone 12 and Its Variants

iPhone 12 Overview

The iPhone 12 is a part of Apple's 14th generation of iPhones. It includes new features such as 5G connectivity, a new design with flat edges, and the powerful A14 Bionic chip.

iPhone 12 Variants

  • iPhone 12 Mini: A smaller version with a 5.4-inch display.
  • iPhone 12: The standard version with a 6.1-inch display.
  • iPhone 12 Pro: Features enhanced cameras and LiDAR for improved AR experiences.
  • iPhone 12 Pro Max: The largest variant with a 6.7-inch display and advanced camera features.
